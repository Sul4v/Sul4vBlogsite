Why did I start this blogging challenge?

There’s a cool story about a pottery class which I found really profound:


A teacher of a pottery class divided her students into two groups. One group who’s going to work on a single pot for 30 days. And another group who’s going to make one pot everyday for 30 days (so at the end, 30 pots).


After the 30 days had passed, the teacher brought all the pots together and judged them on their quality. Every single winner came from the quantity group rather than the quality group.


The lesson is: Repetition > Perfection when learning new skills.


I think this short story answers the above question.
