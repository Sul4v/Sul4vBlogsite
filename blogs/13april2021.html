Letter to your futureself

One of the reasons I have a blogsite is to share what I find fascinating—whether it be ideas or items. Today is no different.



Don’t you think it would be so cool if we could talk to our future self?
We could remind ourselves what we were like in the past or rant about the things that are bothering us at the moment. Or use ourselves as a benchmark for our future-selves.


Well, futureme.org kinda does that. You write a letter (email) to your future-self and you'll get that letter at the specified date in the future.


I just got an email from my 14-year-old self telling me that he doesn’t believe that he is in class 10. One of the lines from the letter was “Class 10, then 12, then Bachelors, life set ho tespaxi!!!”


I’d suggest you take time from your busy schedule and write a letter to your futureself. I can assure you that he/she will definitely love reading the letter. Visit futureme.org


Also, my 14-year-old-self said, “Happy New year!”
