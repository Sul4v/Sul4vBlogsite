Writing

This is the last day of the 1-month-blog-challenge. Here’s a list of what I’ve learned through this process:


1) If you want to accomplish something or build a habit, having an accountability partner helps a lot, even more if there’s money involved.


2) The writing process does get easier eventually. However, I didn’t see a huge improvement in my writing itself. Normally, I’d need at least 3 days to come up with an idea, make the first draft, and rewrite it multiple times. But during this challenge, I had a very limited time so I couldn’t do the rewriting part effectively.


3) One of my main concerns at the beginning was, “What am I going to write about?” But I never ran out of ideas. (There’s a whole list of ideas on my notepad which I’ll work on in the coming days.)


4) Getting things done > Getting things perfect. This mindset alone can take off a lot of pressure of the writing process.


5) Writing blogs was a new thing to me but I’m glad that I tried. This challenge gave a boost in my confidence to try out other new challenges. So yea, don’t be afraid to try out something new every once in a while.
