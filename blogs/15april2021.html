That lil girl

A couple months ago, I was cycling on the streets in Chitwan. A lady was standing on the edge of the road carrying her 4-year-old daughter.
As I rode past her, the little girl waved her hand and said “Byeee.” Her mother looked embarrassed and shushed the little girl.


I looked back at the girl and waved my hand and replied “Byeee.” Both the mother and the daughter gave me a smile which I still vividly remember.


It was an impulsive response. Later I realized that if I hadn’t replied back to that lil girl, chances are she would have never waved to any strangers ever again.
When I look back at that moment, I remind myself of how a small act of kindness can have a massive effect on someone else’s life.
