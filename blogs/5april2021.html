A white shirt guy

Years have passed
realized that
it’s not time, but
moments are what last


He’s lying on a bed
unable to walk
looking at the photographs
and reliving the past.


He had the broadest smile
and dreams so high
a man afraid of nothing
only goal was to touch the sky.


Didn’t have the slightest hint
that he was a white shirt guy
who walked around with dirty people
when he was meant to fly.
